Who I Am

I am a B.Tech AIML student at Parul University (Expected 2028) with a strong passion for Frontend Development. I enjoy creating clean user interfaces and converting ideas into real, functional web pages.

Along with frontend development, I have exposure to cloud computing (AWS), automation tools like n8n, and programming fundamentals in Java, Python, and C. I am constantly learning new technologies and improving my skills to build better and more efficient web applications.
